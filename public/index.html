How to read this dashboard

U-Haul prices one-way truck rentals based on demand. When more people leave a city than arrive, outbound trucks become scarce and prices rise. This tracker compares outbound vs inbound pricing on the same route as a real-time migration signal.

The Migration Pressure Index (MPI) is the ratio of outbound to inbound price for a 15ft truck. An MPI of 2.5× means it costs 2.5x more to leave than to arrive — strong evidence of out-migration demand.

MPI > 1.5× — More people leaving
MPI 0.67–1.5× — Balanced flow
MPI < 0.67× — More people arriving
